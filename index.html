The Reality

You're negotiating alone against suppliers who reward scale.

Every supplier prices on volume. The bigger the buyer, the better the deal. A contractor buying a few million a year gets a fundamentally different price than an organization buying tens of millions. That's not a secret — it's how the business works.

The contractors we're bringing together are already good operators with solid volume. They just lack the collective weight to command the pricing their combined purchases actually deserve. That's what we fix.

Pricing you can't access alone

Suppliers save their best pricing for their biggest buyers. When you buy through United Trades, you are one of their biggest buyers.

Rebate programs on top of the savings

Beyond the better pricing, suppliers pay structured rebates on the group's total volume. We distribute those earnings to you every quarter.

Negotiations handled for you

Supplier pricing negotiations take months of back-and-forth. Our team handles all of it. You focus on running your business.
