The Reality

Your supplier knows exactly what you're worth to them.

Suppliers offer tiered rebate programs — but access depends entirely on volume. A mid-sized construction company buying $1.5M in lumber annually gets a different deal than one buying $15M. That's not a secret. It's policy.

Most of our members qualified perfectly well on their own — they just lacked the leverage to prove it to suppliers who only negotiate with buyers at scale. That's what we fix.

Rebates you didn't know existed

Suppliers run programs they never advertise publicly. You need to be in the room to know about them.

Volume thresholds you can't hit alone

Gold-tier rebate rates often require $5M+ in annual purchases from a single account. Together, we're always there.

Time you don't have to negotiate

Supplier rebate negotiations take months. Our team handles all of it. You just report your purchases.

The Math

Money left on the table is still money.

Adjust the calculator to your volume and your supplier's rebate rate. The math is straightforward — your tier determines how the rebate splits between you and the group, and it updates automatically as your trailing volume grows.

A Silver-tier member doing $1.2M at a 5% rate walks away with $36,000 annually, paid out quarterly. At Gold tier, that climbs to $39,000. The difference is your trailing 12-month purchase volume — no manual tier changes, no negotiation.

Across multiple enrolled supplier programs, members regularly see six-figure annual recoveries on purchases they were already making.

Who We Accept

Not everyone qualifies.
That's the point.

Membership is by application and approval. We're not trying to be the largest group — we're trying to be the best one. Every member we accept either strengthens the group or doesn't make the cut.

01

Qualifying Purchase Volume

Minimum $500,000 in annual purchases across enrolled supplier categories. We verify before extending membership, not after.

02

Established Business

Three or more years operating with verifiable financials. We work with companies that have track records, not startups finding their footing.

03

Geographic Fit

We limit overlapping membership in the same market area. If you're approved, you won't be competing with another member down the street.

04

Construction or Trade Focus

General contractors, specialty subcontractors, building material dealers, and related trade businesses. We know the industry and stay in our lane.
